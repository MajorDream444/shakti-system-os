## What Has Been Built

### 1. Start Your Shakti Path — Public Front Door

A live Vite/React application deployed through Vercel.

Built from the Google AI Studio prototype and organized as the primary app inside the repository. This is the front door into Shakti Shala — a calm discernment experience rather than a sales page, chatbot, or quiz.

**Experience flow:**
Arrival → Recognition → Discernment → Threshold Reveal → Continuation

**Routes women toward one of four pathways:**
- Weekly Shakti Circle
- 1:1 Somatic / Shadow Support
- Shakti Shadow & Somatics Container (6-week)
- Retreat Readiness Pathway

**Key design decisions preserved:**
- Result is shown before contact is requested
- Contact capture is continuation, not a gate
- No urgency, no pressure, no clichés
- The experience is calm, sovereign, and spacious

---

### 2. Shakti Readiness Tracker — Google Form + Google Sheet

A Phase 1 readiness tracker designed to be simple, reviewable, and human-operated.

The Google Form is live and ready to receive seeker information. The Google Sheet acts as the CRM for this phase — no heavy automation, no cold dashboards.

**Tracker tabs:**
- **SEEKERS** — primary view of everyone entering the ecosystem
- **Self-Audit / Form Responses** — preserves original seeker language
- **COMMUNITY MAP** — Bali and online communities where aligned women gather
- **BRIDGE WOMEN** — women who already hold trust: facilitators, teachers, hosts
- **Content Signals** — listening layer for viral clips, comments, DMs, questions
- **METRICS** — simple counts: seekers, sources, pathway interest, signal patterns
