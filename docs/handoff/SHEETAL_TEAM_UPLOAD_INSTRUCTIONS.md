# Sheetal Team Upload Instructions

## Where To Upload

Please upload the first batch of foundational teachings, practices, PDFs, audio, workbooks, transcripts, or journal prompts into:

```text
Shakti Legacy Vault / 10_UPLOAD_HERE_FIRST / 01_RAW_UPLOADS
```

Do not worry about sorting the files into the Temple Library yet.

## What To Upload First

Prioritize low-sensitivity foundational material:

```text
Welcome teachings
Foundational practice PDFs
Short audio reflections
Beginner-friendly meditations
Worksheets
Journal prompts
Retreat preparation notes
Existing transcripts that are ready for review
```

## What Not To Upload As Teaching Content

Keep these separate or clearly marked:

```text
Prototype app archives
Walkthrough videos
Website design exports
Internal build notes
Private client records
Payment records
Raw personal notes not approved for review
```

These can still be uploaded for operations, but they should be marked as internal system assets, not public Shakti teaching content.

## File Naming

Use descriptive names when possible:

```text
foundation-grounding-practice.pdf
durga-breath-audio.mp3
new-moon-reflection-journal-prompt.docx
retreat-arrival-meditation.m4a
```

Avoid names like:

```text
final-final-v3.pdf
untitled.mp3
notes.docx
```

## Review Boundary

Everything uploaded is treated as:

```text
Access Level: Internal
Publishing Status: Needs Review
Confidentiality: Internal
```

The team will review, tag, and route content before anything is approved for the app.

## What Happens Next

The intake process will:

1. Inventory the uploaded files.
2. Draft Vault metadata.
3. Prepare Airtable `Library Assets` records.
4. Route approved files into the Temple Library.
5. Keep the app read-only through `BackendRepository`.

No app publishing happens automatically from upload.
