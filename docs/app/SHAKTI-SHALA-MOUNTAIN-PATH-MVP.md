# Shakti Shala Mountain Path MVP

## Purpose

This document defines the first app-facing MVP for Shakti Shala: a mobile-first digital pilgrimage where women enter through a threshold, ascend a sacred mountain path, move through Tantra micro-teachings, moon rhythms, and goddess gates, and receive an aligned next doorway into Sheetal Kandola's work.

The goal is not addiction.
The goal is readiness.

## Core Thesis

Shakti Shala is a mobile pilgrimage app where the Shakti Shala Sherpa helps women ascend a sacred mountain path through Tantra teachings, moon rhythms, goddess thresholds, and somatic reflections — guiding each seeker toward the doorway her system can actually hold.

## Product Posture

This is not a generic wellness app, a chatbot, a guru simulator, or a spiritual game. It is a sanctuary pathway.

The app should feel like:

- a sacred expedition
- a quiet mobile sanctuary
- a practice map
- a readiness pathway
- a living front door into Shakti Shala

It should not feel like:

- a SaaS dashboard
- a dopamine game
- a therapy bot
- a tantra influencer app
- a content feed
- a conversion funnel

## MVP Journey

```text
Arrival
→ Threshold Quiz
→ Mountain Map
→ ABCs of Tantra Path
→ Moon Rhythm Challenge
→ Goddess Gate
→ Pathway Reveal
→ Recommended Doorway
```

## Core Metaphor

The seeker begins at Base Camp and ascends a spiral mountain path toward Shakti Shala.

The ascent includes:

- The Threshold
- The ABC Path
- The Moon Gate
- The Goddess Spiral
- Practice Chambers
- Retreat Readiness Pass
- Shakti Shala

The app does not reward speed. It rewards rhythm, honesty, grounding, and readiness.

## MVP Screens

### 1. Opening Threshold

A dark Himalayan sanctuary gate. The seeker is invited to begin.

Primary CTA:

```text
Begin the Path
```

Suggested copy:

```text
You do not need to climb the whole mountain today.
Begin with the next steady step.
```

### 2. Entry Discernment

The threshold quiz gathers first-layer readiness signals.

Questions should explore:

- What are you carrying?
- What pace feels supportive?
- What are you longing for?
- What kind of support feels sustainable?
- Are you seeking rhythm, depth, private support, community, or retreat?

### 3. Mountain Map

A spiral pathway up a Himalayan mountain with visible gates:

- Base Camp
- Threshold
- ABC Path
- Moon Gate
- Durga Gate
- Kali Gate
- Lakshmi Gate
- Shakti Shala

For MVP, only three goddess gates are active: Durga, Kali, Lakshmi.

### 4. ABCs of Tantra

Begin with five letters only:

- A — Awareness
- B — Breath
- C — Chaitanya
- D — Dharma
- E — Embodiment

Each lesson contains:

- 1 micro-teaching
- 1 reflection
- 1 body practice
- 1 journal prompt
- 1 pathway signal

### 5. Moon Rhythm Challenge

One New Moon / Full Moon rhythm track.

MVP flow:

```text
Set intention
→ Practice for 3 days
→ Reflect
→ Release
→ Receive next prompt
```

### 6. Goddess Gate 01 — Durga

Theme: foundation, protection, boundaries, steadiness.

Durga is an early gate. She protects the path before intensity.

### 7. Goddess Gate 02 — Kali

Theme: shadow, truth, death-rebirth, cutting illusion.

Kali should not be treated as an automatic first gate. The app should check grounding and support signals before inviting a seeker deeper.

### 8. Goddess Gate 03 — Lakshmi

Theme: receiving, worth, nourishment, beauty, value.

Lakshmi can bridge the seeker toward community rhythm, ceremony, and integration.

### 9. Pathway Reveal

The app recommends one primary doorway and one secondary support option.

Possible recommendations:

- Open Gate
- Moon Rhythm
- Weekly Circle
- 1:1 Support
- 6-Week Container
- Retreat Readiness
- Temple Library

## MVP Readiness Scores

Track five simple scores:

- Rhythm Score
- Shadow Score
- Support Score
- Container Score
- Retreat Readiness Score

Track protective flags:

- Low Capacity
- High Intensity Seeking
- Needs Grounding
- Community-Oriented
- Private Support Needed
- Retreat Fantasy Risk
- Integration Needed

## Sample Unlock Rules

If low capacity and high intensity seeking:

```text
Do not unlock Retreat Readiness immediately.
Recommend Moon Rhythm or Weekly Circle.
```

If shadow interest and grounded capacity:

```text
Unlock Kali Gate and suggest 1:1 Support or the 6-Week Container.
```

If community need and low-pressure support are present:

```text
Recommend Moon Rhythm or Weekly Circle.
```

If retreat longing is high but rhythm is low:

```text
Suggest Retreat Readiness Prep, not direct retreat application.
```

- threshold

## Phase 1 Build Scope

Build only:

- mobile-first threshold experience
- short quiz
- mountain map
- five ABC lessons
- one moon rhythm challenge
- three goddess gates
- pathway reveal
- Sherpa notes

Do not build yet:

- all 13 goddesses
- full native iOS / Android
- full community feed
- payment system
- complex AI companion
- full Temple Library
- live retreat pipeline
