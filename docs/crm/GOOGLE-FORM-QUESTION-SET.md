# Google Form — Question Set

## Start Your Shakti Path Readiness Tracker

This is the 12-question disclosure form that lives behind the "Begin with the Self-Audit" CTA on the deployed site. It is a real act of disclosure, not a marketing survey. It is meant to be read personally by Sheetal or the Shakti Shala team before any outreach occurs.

The form does not mirror the website discernment experience. The website is a short threshold. This form is a deeper application.

---

## Questions

### Q1: Your Name
*Short answer*

What you would like to be called.

---

### Q2: Where are you in the world right now?
*Short answer*

City and country, or region if preferred.

---

### Q3: How do you spend your days?
*Paragraph*

Not a resume. What fills your time, your energy, your attention. Work, caregiving, creative life — whatever is true.

---

### Q4: What has been circling for you — what brought you to this form?
*Paragraph*

What is alive, pressing, or unresolved. What drew you here. You do not need to name it perfectly.

---

### Q5: What practices, paths, or containers have you already been through?
*Paragraph*

Yoga, therapy, somatic work, courses, circles, retreats — anything that has shaped you or left you wanting more.

---

### Q6: What has hit its limit — or what hasn't been enough?
*Paragraph*

Where have previous experiences fallen short? What have you outgrown? What do you sense is still waiting?

---

### Q7: What feels most alive for you right now — what are you most ready to move toward?
*Paragraph*

This is not about goals. What direction is your body or spirit already leaning toward?

---

### Q8: Is there anything — practical, logistical, emotional — that might make it harder to show up fully for deeper work right now?
*Paragraph*

Constraints, fears, competing demands, life circumstances. Honesty here protects you and the container.

---

### Q9: Are you currently working with a therapist, somatic practitioner, or other healing support?
*Short answer or paragraph*

Yes / No / Sometimes. Who or what kind of support, if you want to share.

---

### Q10: What kind of container are you drawn to right now?
*Multiple choice or paragraph*

Options (adapt to actual Google Form setup):
- 1:1 sessions with Sheetal
- Weekly Shakti Circle (group)
- 9-Session Shakti Shadow & Somatics Container
- Retreat or immersion (Bali / sacred travel)
- I am open and want to be guided

---

### Q11: What is your honest capacity for investment — in time and financially — right now?
*Paragraph*

Not what you wish was true. What is actually true right now. This question is not a filter — it is an invitation to be honest with yourself.

---

### Q12: Is there anything you want Sheetal to know before she reads this?
*Paragraph*

Anything that doesn't fit the questions above. A tone, a context, a feeling — or nothing at all.

---

### Email Address
*Email field*

Required for Sheetal to reach out after reviewing your form.

---

## Routing

Form responses are captured in two places:

1. **Self-Audit / Form Responses tab** — full verbatim archive, question by question
2. **SEEKERS tab** — normalized summary row for human review

The `Recommended Pathway` field in SEEKERS is populated by mapping Q10 (container preference) to the four pathway keys using the Apps Script `mapContainerToPathway_()` function. This is an approximate mapping — Sheetal always has final discernment.

---

## What Happens After Submission

Nothing automated. Sheetal or the team reads the SEEKERS tab. They read the full disclosure in the Self-Audit / Form Responses tab. They reach out personally when they are ready to hold that conversation.
