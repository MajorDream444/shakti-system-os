# Prompt 01 — Master Build Prompt

```text
Build a premium, cinematic, mobile-first interactive web experience called:

“Start Your Shakti Path”

This is the one-shot MVP prototype for the Shakti System OS front door, created for Sheetal Kandola’s body of work, Shakti Shadow & Somatics.

This is NOT:
- a chatbot
- a SaaS dashboard
- a marketing funnel
- a generic wellness landing page
- a tantra influencer page
- a clinical therapy app

This IS:
- a calm digital threshold
- a guided first step
- a sanctuary-like discernment experience
- a structured but emotionally spacious pathfinding moment
- the first doorway into a deeper ecosystem of circles, 1:1 support, containers, and retreat readiness

The emotional goal:
A woman arrives uncertain or curious. The experience does not sell to her. It steadies her. It gives language to what she may be carrying. It helps her discern the doorway that may fit. It leaves her with one clear next step.

Core emotional instruction:

Steady first.
Orient second.
Route third.

Visual thesis:
Natural sanctuary on the surface. Precision architecture beneath.

Design direction:
- dark sanctuary mode as the main experience world
- obsidian and temple-charcoal background field
- deep burgundy and clay-toned surfaces
- ember/fire accent glows in burnt orange and warm copper
- warm stone and ash-ivory text tones
- subtle water/current/pathway linework to balance fire with flow
- temple threshold / sacred doorway atmosphere
- Himalayan monastery stillness and old-stone gravitas, but no cliché ritual overload
- refined editorial presence, not boho collage
- front-facing warmth with hidden compositional precision

Reference inheritance:
Borrow from high-end cinematic design references:
- dark void field
- centered emotional focal object
- controlled ember linework
- layered atmospheric depth
- soft glow and restraint
- interface rhythm without dashboard identity

Reject:
- startup tech visuals
- visible metrics / system dashboards
- neon cyberpunk
- generic meditation app pastels
- overused mandalas
- stock spiritual gradients
- social-media retreat clichés
- arbitrary glassmorphism

Typography:
- refined serif display headlines, elegant but not theatrical
- clean humanist sans-serif body text and interaction labels
- large whitespace
- few words on screen at once
- body copy should remain highly readable

Motion philosophy:
- breath
- ember
- current
- ascent

Use subtle motion:
- slow glow pulses
- masked heading reveals
- gentle fade and rise transitions
- low-frequency ambient texture drift
- warm path illumination on reveal screens
- soft hover lift on cards

Avoid:
- aggressive parallax
- twitchy animation
- particle storms
- constant moving clutter
- gamified quiz transitions

The experience structure must have 8 screens:

SCREEN 1 — THE THRESHOLD
Headline:
“Begin your Shakti path with structure your body can trust.”

Support line:
“This is a calm first step into Sheetal Kandola’s work — rooted in Shakti Tantra, somatics, shadow integration, and nervous-system literacy.”

Small grounding line:
“You do not need to know exactly where to begin. We will move slowly.”

Primary CTA:
“Enter the Threshold”

Visual:
A sacred doorway / threshold-like focal object. Warm interior ember light. Ambient stillness. This should feel like arrival, not a sales page.

SCREEN 2 — ORIENTATION
Headline:
“Not every woman needs the same doorway.”

Body copy:
“Some women are seeking rhythm.
Some need focused support.
Some are ready for deeper container work.
Some feel called toward retreat, but want to enter with care.

This short experience helps you sense what kind of support may be most aligned now.”

Microcopy:
“There are no right answers. Choose what feels most true.”

CTA:
“Continue”

SCREEN 3 — RECOGNITION CHOICE
Prompt:
“Which of these feels closest to where you are right now?”

Choice cards:
1. “I’ve been carrying too much and need steadier rhythm.”
2. “I want focused support for what I’m moving through.”
3. “I’m ready for deeper personal work with structure.”
4. “I feel drawn toward retreat or immersion, but I want to discern it carefully.”

Design note:
These should feel like selecting a doorway, not answering a survey.

SCREEN 4 — BODY / PACE CHECK
Prompt:
“What kind of pace feels most supportive right now?”

Choice cards:
1. “Gentle and ongoing”
2. “Personal and focused”
3. “Structured and committed”
4. “Immersive, but only with real readiness”

SCREEN 5 — DEPTH PREFERENCE
Prompt:
“What are you most seeking from this work?”

Choice cards:
1. “Community and consistency”
2. “Clarity around a personal threshold”
3. “Shadow and somatic integration”
4. “A deeper initiation into retreat-level experience”

SCREEN 6 — OPTIONAL OPEN REFLECTION
Prompt:
“In a few words, what has brought you here?”

Textarea placeholder:
“You can keep this simple. A sentence is enough.”

This should be optional, not required.

SCREEN 7 — THE THRESHOLD REVEAL
Route to one of four recommended pathways based on the selected cards:
- Weekly Shakti Circle
- 1:1 Somatic / Shadow Support
- 6-Week Shakti Shadow & Somatics Container
- Retreat Readiness Pathway

Use a simple local scoring logic in the prototype. Each selected answer should contribute to one or more pathway scores. The open reflection may inform the generated reflection text if AI is used, but it should not override the chosen pathway in the MVP.

For each result screen, include:
- a headline
- a short reflection paragraph
- recommended next step title
- one primary CTA
- one secondary CTA

Use these exact result headlines:

Weekly Circle:
“Your clearest doorway may be rhythm.”

1:1:
“Your clearest doorway may be focused support.”

6-Week Container:
“Your clearest doorway may be structured depth.”

Retreat:
“Retreat may be calling — but readiness matters.”

Result language guidelines:
- mirror, do not diagnose
- preserve agency
- use “may,” “could,” “seems aligned,” “consider”
- do not claim certainty
- do not imitate Sheetal
- do not act like a healer or therapist

Suggested pathway copy:

Weekly Circle reflection:
“What you selected suggests that steadiness, recurring touchpoints, and a lower-pressure way to enter the work may be most supportive now.”
Recommended next step:
“Weekly Shakti Circle”
CTA:
“Explore the Weekly Circle”
Secondary CTA:
“Take the Deeper Self-Audit”

CTA:
“Explore 1:1 Support”
Secondary CTA:
“Take the Deeper Self-Audit”

Recommended next step:
“6-Week Shakti Shadow & Somatics Container”

“Learn About the Container”
Secondary CTA:
“Take the Deeper Self-Audit”

Recommended next step:
“Retreat Readiness Pathway”
CTA:
“See if Retreat Is Aligned”
Secondary CTA:
“Begin with the Self-Audit”

Visual direction for result screen:
A warm path has now appeared. The user should feel like a doorway has opened, not like they received a lead score.

SCREEN 8 — WARM HANDOFF

- First name

Buttons:
- “Send My Path”

Behavioral guardrails:
- no manipulative urgency
- no countdown timers
- no conversion language
- no “divine feminine” cliché phrases
- no “healing journey” filler
- no “step into your power” copy
- no “transform your life instantly” copy
- no fake clinical certainty
- no AI pretending to be Sheetal
- no diagnosing users
- no trauma-mining

Use Sheetal-specific language posture:
- clinical-embodied clarity for instructions, prompts, and CTAs
- poetic-precise restraint for major threshold lines and reveal moments

Expected prototype output:
- a polished, interactive, responsive single-page app or multi-screen flow
- strong first-screen atmosphere
- functional interaction between screens
- local state management for pathway routing
- elegant choice cards
- emotionally strong result reveal
- handoff form mockup
- high-quality mobile responsiveness

Do not overbuild production integrations yet. This is a one-shot MVP prototype, not a final deployable system.
```

# Suggested Local Routing Logic for MVP

This is a simple reference logic. AI Studio may implement its own equivalent, but the expected idea is:

```text
Question 1:
- rhythm answer → +2 circle
- focused support answer → +2 one_to_one
- structured personal work answer → +2 container
- retreat discernment answer → +2 retreat

Question 2:
- gentle ongoing → +1 circle
- personal focused → +1 one_to_one
- structured committed → +1 container
- immersive with readiness → +1 retreat

Question 3:
- community consistency → +2 circle
- personal threshold clarity → +2 one_to_one
- shadow/somatic integration → +2 container
- retreat-level experience → +2 retreat

Winner = highest score.
Tie handling:
- Circle wins over other options only if first question signaled overwhelm/rhythm.
- 1:1 wins tie when personal threshold language dominates.
- Container wins tie when structured depth is selected twice.
- Retreat wins tie only when retreat is selected directly in Question 1 and supported by another retreat-leaning answer.
```
